Introducing Class 1-A

Class 1-A Homeroom Teacher

Good day, everyone. I’m Shota Aizawa, but you may know me as Eraser Head. I’m the homeroom teacher for Class 1-A at U.A. High School. My role is to prepare these aspiring heroes for the challenges they'll face in the real world.


I value practicality and resilience in my students, pushing them to develop their skills and understand the importance of teamwork. U.A. High is a unique institution that fosters growth and innovation in the hero community, and I'm proud to be a part of it.


If you see my students out there, remember—each one of them is working hard to become the heroes you can rely on. Together, we’re shaping the future of hero society. Allow me to introduce you to the future heroes of Class 1-A!

CLASS 1-A HEROES

Class 1-A Students

Tenya Iida (Ingenium)

Greetings! I am Tenya Iida, also known as Ingenium. My Quirk, Engine, grants me super speed by using engines in my legs. I come from the prestigious Iida family, known for producing heroes, and I am dedicated to upholding their legacy with honor and diligence.


As your class representative, I am committed to ensuring that we maintain discipline and order during our training. I believe that being a hero is about responsibility, and I’m excited to lead by example. Together, we can achieve our goals and become true heroes!

Izuku Midoriya (Deku)

Hi, everyone! I’m Izuku Midoriya, but you can call me Deku! Ever since I was a kid, I've dreamed of becoming a hero like All Might, the Symbol of Peace. I may not have had a Quirk when I was younger, but that never stopped me from studying heroes and training hard. Now, thanks to my mentor, All Might, I inherited his Quirk, One For All, and I’m determined to make my dreams come true!


I believe that being a hero is about more than just strength; it's about saving people and inspiring hope. I’m really excited to be in Class 1-A with all of you, and I look forward to learning from each of you. Let's work together and become the best heroes we can be!

Katsuki Bakugo (Great Explosion Murder God: Dynamight)

Listen up! I’m Katsuki Bakugo, also known as Explosion! With my Quirk, I can create massive blasts of sweat from my palms, allowing me to unleash powerful attacks. I’ve trained hard to master my abilities, and I’m not going to let anyone stand in my way. I’ve always been competitive, so don’t think you can keep up with me!


I have a strong sense of pride and a clear vision of what I want to achieve. I may come off as abrasive or aggressive, but I respect those who are willing to work hard and improve themselves. So, bring your best game because I’m here to prove I’m the best of the best!

Shoto Todoroki (Shoto)

Hello, I’m Shoto Todoroki. I possess a unique Quirk that allows me to control both ice and fire, inherited from my parents. While my father, Endeavor, is a renowned hero, I’ve struggled with my identity and the expectations that come with my heritage. I aim to forge my own path and become a great hero in my own right.


I may not be the most talkative person, but I value hard work and determination. I’m eager to learn from my classmates and grow stronger alongside all of you. Together, I hope we can push each other to new heights and truly become heroes worthy of our dreams.

Ochaco Uraraka (Uravity)

Hi there! I’m Ochaco Uraraka, but you can just call me Uravity! My Quirk is Zero Gravity, which lets me make objects float. I want to be a hero not just to help people but also to support my family financially. Becoming a Pro Hero is my dream, and I’ll work hard to make it happen!


I’m friendly and optimistic, and I believe that teamwork is essential in hero work. I’m excited to get to know all of you and face challenges together. Let’s support each other and have a great time while we train to be the heroes the world needs!
