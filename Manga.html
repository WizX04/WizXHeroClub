Manga

Volume 1: Izuku Midoriya: Origin

Izuku Midoriya dreams of becoming a hero in a world where nearly everyone has superpowers (called Quirks), but he was born without one. His life changes when he meets his idol, All Might, and inherits his Quirk, "One For All." Midoriya begins his journey to become a hero by enrolling in U.A. High School.

Volume 2: Rage, You Damned Nerd

Midoriya starts his training at U.A. alongside his new classmates. The class engages in a mock battle where he faces off against his childhood friend-turned-bully, Katsuki Bakugo. Midoriya struggles to control his Quirk, but manages to win with strategy.

Volume 3: All Might

The students are thrown into a battle scenario at the Unforeseen Simulation Joint (USJ) when real villains attack. The League of Villains, led by Tomura Shigaraki, tries to kill All Might. Midoriya and his classmates fight to survive while All Might arrives to save them.

Volume 4: The Boy Born with Everything

The U.A. Sports Festival begins, showcasing the students' abilities in front of pro heroes. Midoriya's new rivals, such as the prodigy Shoto Todoroki, emerge. Todoroki’s power, a combination of fire and ice, makes him one of the strongest contenders.

Volume 5: Shoto Todoroki: Origin

Todoroki confronts his troubled past as the son of the hero Endeavor, using only his ice powers in an attempt to reject his father. Midoriya challenges him to embrace both sides of his Quirk during their fight in the Sports Festival, pushing Todoroki to use his firepower.

Volume 6: Struggling

The festival concludes with Bakugo's victory, though he's angry at how it ended. Afterward, students choose pro heroes for internships. Midoriya meets Gran Torino, a retired hero, who helps him better control One For All. The League of Villains plots in the background.

Volume 7: Katsuki Bakugo: Origin

The internship arc heats up as Midoriya battles the hero killer Stain, who has a dangerous ideology about true heroes. With help from Todoroki and Tenya Iida, they manage to subdue Stain. Meanwhile, Bakugo struggles with his desire to surpass Midoriya and All Might.

Volume 8: Yaoyorozu Rising

Midoriya returns to school as final exams approach. The students must face their teachers in combat. Midoriya teams up with Bakugo to face All Might in a training exercise, testing their teamwork despite their rivalry. Other students, such as Momo Yaoyorozu, show their growth.

Volume 9: My Hero

The internship arc heats up as Midoriya battles the hero killer Stain, who has a dangerous ideology about true heroes. With help from Todoroki and Tenya Iida, they manage to subdue Stain. Meanwhile, Bakugo struggles with his desire to surpass Midoriya and All Might.

Volume 10: All for One

The villains successfully kidnap Bakugo. Midoriya, Todoroki, Iida, and others plan a rescue mission, but they struggle against the powerful villain All For One, who has the ability to steal and give Quirks. All Might faces off against All For One in a climactic battle.

Volume 11: End of the Beginning, Beginning of the End

All Might defeats All For One, but his power fades, forcing him into retirement. He passes the torch fully to Midoriya. Bakugo learns the truth about Midoriya's Quirk. The world reacts to the Symbol of Peace's retirement, and society faces an uncertain future.

Volume 12: The Test

With All Might gone, U.A. introduces new security measures, including dormitories. The students prepare for their Provisional Hero License Exam. Midoriya continues to improve his control over One For All, while Bakugo and Todoroki face challenges of their own during the test.

Volume 13: A Talk About Your Quirk

The Hero License Exam continues, testing teamwork and strategic thinking. Midoriya starts developing a new fighting style after learning more about One For All. Meanwhile, Shigaraki begins recruiting more villains, aiming to destabilize society even further.

Volume 14: Overhaul

A new villain, Overhaul, emerges and begins to execute a dangerous plan involving a mysterious girl named Eri, whose Quirk can erase others' powers. The heroes, including Midoriya, team up with pro heroes like Sir Nighteye to stop Overhaul's growing threat.

Volume 15: Fighting Fate

The heroes infiltrate Overhaul’s base to rescue Eri. Mirio, one of U.A.’s strongest students, and Midoriya take on Overhaul in an intense battle. Sir Nighteye’s ability to see the future complicates matters as he predicts dire outcomes for the mission.

Volume 16: Red Riot

The battle against Overhaul intensifies, with Kirishima (Red Riot) stepping up in a key fight. Midoriya unlocks more of One For All’s potential to defeat Overhaul and save Eri. This victory comes at a cost, with heavy emotional and physical tolls on the heroes.

Volume 17: Lemillion

Mirio, also known as Lemillion, showcases his incredible power in his fight against Overhaul but suffers devastating consequences. The heroes finally take down Overhaul, and Midoriya's bond with Eri grows as they try to heal the trauma she's endured.

Volume 18: Bright Future

After the battle with Overhaul, the students return to their daily routines. However, they must deal with the aftermath of the battle and the emotional fallout. Midoriya learns more about One For All’s connection to the previous users of the Quirk.

Volume 19: School Festival

U.A. prepares for its annual School Festival. Class 1-A organizes a performance to lift everyone's spirits. Meanwhile, a new villain named Gentle Criminal attempts to infiltrate U.A., and Midoriya faces him in a battle of ideals.

Volume 20: Deku vs. Gentle Criminal

Midoriya faces off against Gentle Criminal, who seeks to prove his worth through his villainous acts. Despite the intensity of their fight, Midoriya manages to defeat him, allowing the School Festival to proceed without major incident.

Volume 21: Why He Gets Back Up

After the festival, the students focus on their work-study programs, and Midoriya trains with pro hero Endeavor. The stakes grow higher as Midoriya uncovers more about the One For All Quirk, learning he has access to the powers of its previous users.

Volume 22: That Which Is Inherited

Endeavor and Hawks, the new top heroes, investigate the growing threat of the Paranormal Liberation Front. Midoriya continues his training under Endeavor, grappling with his new powers and the evolving dangers surrounding the League of Villains.

Volume 23: Tomura Shigaraki: Origin

The spotlight shifts to Shigaraki, revealing his dark past and the origins of his hatred toward heroes. As he gains more power, the League of Villains forms a new alliance, preparing to wage war on hero society.

Volume 24: All It Takes Is One Bad Day

The League of Villains continues to grow, and Shigaraki reaches terrifying new levels of power. The heroes prepare for an all-out war against the Paranormal Liberation Front. Midoriya and his classmates brace for the fight of their lives.

Volume 25: War

The heroes launch a full-scale attack on the Paranormal Liberation Front's headquarters. As the battle rages on, Midoriya and the others face overwhelming opposition. Shigaraki awakens with catastrophic power, threatening to destroy everything in his path.

Volume 26: The High, Deep Blue Sky

The war between heroes and villains escalates. The heroes fight valiantly, but the tide begins to turn in the villains' favor as Shigaraki's destructive Quirk wreaks havoc. Several pro heroes face devastating losses.

Volume 27: One's Justice

The heroes’ forces are spread thin as Shigaraki’s power grows. Midoriya begins to understand the true danger of Shigaraki, who now possesses the enhanced powers of All For One. The battle's aftermath leaves society in chaos.

Volume 28: Crisis

The world begins to crumble as public trust in heroes falters. Midoriya takes on a more proactive role in trying to protect civilians, while the League of Villains prepares for its next move, aiming to plunge the world into even deeper disorder.

Volume 29: Bakugo Rising

The focus shifts to Bakugo, who begins to come to terms with his rivalry and friendship with Midoriya. In the midst of chaos, Bakugo fights alongside Midoriya, acknowledging their complex relationship as they confront Shigaraki's forces.

Volume 30: Deku vs. Class 1-A

Midoriya leaves U.A. and tries to shoulder the burden of protecting everyone alone. However, his friends in Class 1-A refuse to let him face the darkness by himself. They confront him, determined to bring him back home.

Volume 31: The Ones Within Us

Midoriya returns to U.A. with his classmates' support. Meanwhile, the League of Villains, led by Shigaraki and All For One, continue to amass power. Midoriya learns more about the previous users of One For All and prepares for the final showdown.

Volume 32: Threads of Hope

The heroes regroup and prepare for the inevitable clash with the villains. Tensions rise as they strategize their final stand against the Paranormal Liberation Front. Midoriya resolves to save Shigaraki despite everything that’s happened.

Volume 33: Let It Flow! School Festival

The students organize one last event—a festival—to boost morale before the looming battle. While small in scale, this event reminds them of their humanity and friendship. But the final war against the League of Villains looms on the horizon.

Volume 34: Villains and Heroes

The final battle between heroes and villains begins. The students of U.A. take on major roles in the fight as they face down the League of Villains. Midoriya and Shigaraki confront each other as the fate of hero society hangs in the balance.

Volume 35: Battle in the Sky

As the battle rages, Midoriya and Shigaraki take their fight to the sky. Their clash embodies the ultimate struggle between hope and despair. Both sides suffer devastating losses as the fight reaches its peak.

Volume 36: Deku’s Dark Path

Midoriya struggles with the weight of his responsibilities as the final battle stretches on. He begins to lose hope, but his friends and allies continue to push him forward. Shigaraki's power threatens to overwhelm everything in its path.

Volume 37: Shigaraki’s Awakening

Shigaraki taps into the full potential of All For One, reaching an unimaginable level of power. Midoriya pushes himself to the limit, desperately searching for a way to stop Shigaraki and save the world from destruction.

Volume 38: Final Gamble

In a last-ditch effort to stop Shigaraki, Midoriya unleashes the full power of One For All. The stakes have never been higher as both sides prepare for their final moves. Allies and enemies alike face their destinies in the ultimate confrontation.

Volume 39: Hope

The final volume (to date) centers on the final moments of the war between the heroes and the League of Villains. Midoriya and Shigaraki’s fates are decided in an epic, emotional climax that will change the world forever.
