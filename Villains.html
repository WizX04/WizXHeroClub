Villains

Do you know the villains that threaten humanity in this world?


The Shie Hassaikai is a dangerous yakuza organization that was almost forgotten, but now it’s back—stronger than ever—under the leadership of a villain named Overhaul. He’s using the group to create quirk-erasing bullets, and he’s exploiting a little girl named Eri to do it. It breaks my heart to think about what she’s going through. She’s so young, and her quirk, Rewind, is incredible, but it’s being used for such a terrible purpose.


Overhaul is terrifying—his quirk, Overhaul, lets him break apart anything and put it back together however he wants. He’s surrounded by some of the toughest fighters in the group called the Eight Bullets. There’s Rappa, a powerful fighter, and Chronostasis, Overhaul’s right-hand man, who can slow down anyone with his quirk.


But the Shie Hassaikai isn’t the only threat we face. There are other villains lurking in the shadows, each with their own sinister motives, ready to challenge our ideals and disrupt society. They’re aiming to take down hero society and restore the power of the underworld, and I can’t let that happen. We have to stop them and save Eri from this nightmare before it’s too late.

Shie Hassaikai

Overhaul

Quirk: Overhaul

I am Kai Chisaki, but you know me as Overhaul. I lead the Shie Hassaikai, and my quirk, also called Overhaul, gives me the power to disassemble and reassemble anything I touch. In this world, I see only one path forward: a world free of heroes and filled with my vision of order. I will do whatever it takes to achieve my goals, even if it means using innocent lives as pawns in my game.

Kendo Rappa

Quirk: Strongarm

Name’s Rappa, and I’m one of Overhaul’s top fighters. My quirk, Strongarm, lets me unleash rapid punches that’ll knock you off your feet before you even see me coming. I love the thrill of a good fight, and I can’t wait to see how far I can push myself against a real hero. Just remember, once you step into the ring with me, there’s no turning back.

Hari Kurono

Quirk: Chronostasis

I’m Chronostasis, and I serve as Overhaul’s right-hand man. My quirk allows me to slow down anyone I touch, making them feel like they’re moving through molasses. It gives me the advantage to immobilize opponents before they even realize what’s happening. I’m here to ensure that our plans go smoothly, and anyone who gets in our way will regret it.

Curious about Eri? Take a moment to dive in and discover the delightful essence of who she truly is!

Eri

Quirk: Rewind

Um... I’m Eri. I’m just a girl, but I have a quirk called Rewind. It lets me... um, rewind things to a previous state. Sometimes, I wish I could use it to go back to a time when everything was okay. I don’t really know how to control it, and it’s scary. I’ve been taken away from my home and forced to do things I don’t want to do. It’s hard to explain how lonely it feels.

Eri

Quirk: Rewind

But I’ve heard stories about heroes. They fight for what’s right and protect people. I want to be brave like them. I want to help them save everyone, including myself. I know I have this power inside me, and even though it’s really hard, I want to learn how to use it for good. I believe that if I can control my quirk, I can make a difference.

Eri

Quirk: Rewind

I want to be strong and help the heroes one day. I believe they will come for me and that I won’t be alone forever. I just hope they find me soon because I want to feel safe and loved again.

I also want to warn you that there’s a far more dangerous organization than the Shie Hassaikai!


It’s also important to know who the League of Villains are. They’re one of the most dangerous organizations we face, united by their desire to dismantle hero society and spread chaos. Their leader is Tomura Shigaraki, and other key members include Kurogiri, Dabi, Toga Himiko, Twice, Mr. Compress, Spinner, and All For One.


The League of Villains operates in secrecy, constantly plotting against heroes and society itself. Each of them has their own motivations, but together they pose a significant threat to peace. Their actions are unpredictable, making them all the more dangerous.


We must be ready to face them at any moment, as they seek to undermine everything we stand for as heroes. Every encounter with them reminds me of the importance of standing strong and never giving up. We will protect our society and fight against the darkness they represent.

League of Villains

Tomura Shigaraki

Quirk: Decay

I am Tomura Shigaraki, the leader of the League of Villains. With my quirk, Decay, I can disintegrate anything I touch. I’m driven by a desire to see this society crumble, as I believe it’s rotten to the core. Heroes are nothing but a facade, and I’ll stop at nothing to expose that truth.

We are the League of Villains, and we will tear down the world of heroes!

Kurogiri

Quirk: Warp

I am Kurogiri, the strategist of the League. My quirk, Warp Gate, allows me to create portals for our movements, making us difficult to catch. I am dedicated to ensuring our plans run smoothly and will protect my comrades at all costs. My loyalty to the League is unwavering, and I will do whatever it takes to achieve our goals.

Dabi

Quirk: Blue Flame

Name’s Dabi. My quirk, Blue Flame, burns hotter than any fire you’ve ever seen. I thrive on chaos and the thrill of battle, especially when it involves heroes who think they’re invincible. I have my own scores to settle, and I can’t wait to watch them burn.

Himiko Toga

Quirk: Transform

Hehe! I’m Himiko Toga, and I love the thrill of battle. With my quirk, Transform, I can take on the appearance of anyone by drinking their blood. The chaos that ensues in a fight is exhilarating, especially when heroes are involved. I just want to have fun and enjoy every moment of it!

Twice

Quirk: Double

Hey, I’m Twice! With my quirk, Double, I can create clones of myself, which can be quite a surprise for our enemies. It’s a lot of fun, and I love to mess with people’s heads. Just remember, when you think you’ve defeated me, there’s likely more than one of me out there!

Mr. Compress

Quirk: Compress

Ah, how delightful! I’m Mr. Compress, and my quirk allows me to compress people and objects into marbles. I enjoy the thrill of a good heist and the theatrics that come with it. When I capture a hero, it’s not just a victory; it’s a performance!

Spinner

Quirk: Gecko

Yo, I’m Spinner! My quirk, Gecko, gives me the ability to climb walls and grants me reptilian traits. I’m here to support the League and fight for what I believe in. I may not be the strongest, but I’m determined to prove my worth.

Muscular

Quirk: Muscle Augmentation

Hey there, I’m Muscular, and I’m all about raw power! My quirk, Muscle Augmentation, lets me amplify my strength to insane levels, making me a true powerhouse. I thrive on fighting strong opponents, and I can’t resist the thrill of a good brawl. Heroes don’t stand a chance against me, and I’m here to prove that strength is everything!

All For One

Quirk: All For One

I am All For One, the mastermind behind many of the League’s plans. My power to steal quirks from others makes me a force to be reckoned with. All Might, your time is running out, and soon the world will see the true face of fear. I will dismantle everything you stand for and replace it with my vision of chaos.
