Introducing Class 1-B

Class 1-B Homeroom Teacher

Hello there, citizens! I’m Sekijiro Kan, or as I’m known in the hero world, Vlad King. I’m the homeroom teacher for Class 1-B at U.A. High School. My job is to train the next generation of heroes, helping them unlock their potential and protect society. You might be familiar with Class 1-A, but trust me—Class 1-B is full of incredible talent too. With my guidance, they’ll be ready to step up and face any challenge.


What I love about U.A. High is its commitment to pushing both students and teachers to their limits. This school gives every one of us the tools we need to grow, no matter our quirks. The diversity and drive here are unmatched.


And if you want to know what true potential looks like, get to know my students in Class 1-B. Each one of them is a future hero, ready to protect and serve you with everything they've got. Rest assured, we’re preparing heroes who will keep you safe and secure!
