Welcome to UA High School

Overview of UA High School

Greetings, students! I am Principal Nezu, the brilliant and highly esteemed leader of UA High School, officially known as U.A. (Yuuei) High School. Our academy is renowned throughout Japan as a prestigious institution specializing in the education and development of young heroes. Established to shape the heroes of tomorrow, UA High School is recognized for its rigorous curriculum, blending academic excellence with intense physical training. We focus on helping students master their Quirks—those remarkable superpowers that set them apart. Located in the city of Musutafu, UA has built a strong reputation for producing some of society’s most skilled and celebrated heroes. Now, let us turn our attention to one of our most promising young heroes—Deku. Let’s hear what he has to say!

Hey there, it's me again, Deku!

I bet you're all curious about why I chose to apply to this prestigious school, right? Well, all your questions will be answered as you explore the next few pages of this website. But before you move forward, there's someone important I'd like you to meet—our incredible senseis, the heroes who guide us through every challenge.


Take a moment to get to know them before diving into the rest of the site!

Shota Aizawa

Eraser Head always seems so tired and serious, but I know he cares about us. He has this incredible Quirk that can erase others' abilities, which is both intimidating and awe-inspiring. He pushes us hard, but it’s clear he wants us to be the best heroes we can be. Even though he doesn’t show it often, his support makes me feel more confident.

Toshinori Yagi

All Might is everything I aspire to be! His smile brings hope to everyone, and he’s always encouraging me to do my best. He taught me that true heroism isn’t just about power but about saving people with a smile. Even when he’s not in his hero form, his words inspire me to push through my limits and keep fighting for what’s right.



Hizashi Yamada

Present Mic is so energetic! His voice can fill the whole classroom, and he really knows how to get us pumped up for class. He brings a fun and exciting atmosphere, which makes learning about heroes feel like an adventure. I admire how he genuinely cares about us and wants to help us grow as heroes, especially in how he always encourages us to express ourselves.

Nemuri Kayama

Midnight sensei is so cool and confident! At first, I was a little intimidated by her style, but she really knows how to connect with us. She helps us understand the importance of strategy and psychology in hero work. Her classes can be intense, but she always makes sure we learn something valuable. I admire her creativity and her ability to make tough lessons enjoyable.

Teachers at UA High School

Shota Aizawa (Eraser Head)

Shota Aizawa, commonly referred to as Eraser Head, is the homeroom teacher for Class 1-A at U.A. High School in the world of My Hero Academia. He possesses a unique Quirk known as Erasure, which allows him to nullify the Quirks of others simply by making eye contact with them, making him a formidable opponent in any confrontation. Aizawa is often characterized by his unkempt black hair, dark circles under his eyes, and a perpetually tired expression, reflecting his nocturnal habits and dedication to his work.


Despite his laid-back demeanor and tendency to appear apathetic, Aizawa is deeply committed to his students' growth and safety. He is strict when it comes to training and evaluations, pushing his students to their limits to prepare them for the challenges of being heroes. His approach to teaching emphasizes the importance of hard work, resilience, and teamwork, and he often shows a softer side, particularly when it comes to his students' well-being. Aizawa's protective nature and quick-thinking make him a respected mentor, and he often uses his Erasure Quirk strategically during battles to level the playing field.

Toshinori Yagi (All Might)

All Might, known as Toshinori Yagi, is the former No. 1 hero and the enduring symbol of peace in the world of My Hero Academia. His Quirk, One For All, not only grants him immense strength and speed but also allows him to transfer his power to others, making him a vital mentor to aspiring heroes. With his iconic muscular physique and cheerful smile, All Might inspires hope and courage in everyone around him. As a part-time instructor at U.A. High School, he is beloved by students and faculty alike for his unwavering spirit and dedication to the principles of heroism.


Despite his larger-than-life persona, All Might grapples with the limitations of his power and the toll it takes on his body. This duality adds depth to his character, as he balances his responsibilities as a symbol of hope with the reality of his vulnerability. Compassionate and attentive, he strives to instill in his students the values necessary to become true heroes, reminding them of the importance of using their powers for the greater good. Through his guidance, All Might leaves a lasting impact, encouraging his students to embrace their unique qualities and strive for greatness.


Hizashi Yamada (Present Mic)

Hizashi Yamada, widely known as Present Mic, is a dynamic and enthusiastic teacher at U.A. High School, where he passionately instructs Language Arts. His Quirk, Voice, allows him to amplify his voice to tremendous levels, making him an engaging and captivating presence in the classroom. With his energetic personality and love for music, Present Mic encourages his students to embrace the power of communication, emphasizing that strong communication skills are essential for effective heroism.


As a mentor, Present Mic is dedicated to nurturing the talents of his students and instilling in them the confidence to express themselves. His vibrant approach to teaching not only makes learning enjoyable but also highlights the importance of teamwork and understanding in the world of heroes. With a natural ability to motivate and inspire, Present Mic plays a crucial role in shaping the next generation of heroes, reminding them that their voices can be powerful tools for change.

Nemuri Kayama (Midnight)

Nemuri Kayama, commonly known as Midnight, is the school nurse and a hero at U.A. High School, celebrated for her unique Quirk, Sleep, which allows her to induce sleep in others. With her playful and teasing personality, she brings a vibrant energy to the school environment. Midnight teaches various subjects, often incorporating elements of psychological well-being into her lessons. Her approach emphasizes the importance of mental health in the demanding world of hero training, encouraging students to take care of their emotional well-being as much as their physical abilities.


As an advocate for mental health awareness, Midnight recognizes the pressures faced by aspiring heroes and actively promotes open discussions about mental health issues. She provides a supportive atmosphere where students feel comfortable sharing their struggles and seeking guidance. Through her engaging teaching style and genuine care for her students, Midnight plays a vital role in shaping well-rounded heroes who are not only strong in their abilities but also resilient in their minds.
