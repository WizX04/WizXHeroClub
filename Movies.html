My Hero Academia Movies

My Hero Academia: Two Heroes

Year Released: 2018

Set between seasons 2 and 3 of the anime, Two Heroes follows Izuku Midoriya and All Might as they travel to "I-Island," a man-made floating city where the world's leading scientists study Quirks and develop new hero technology. All Might reunites with an old friend, Dr. David Shield, who created his hero costume. However, the island is soon attacked by villains led by the mysterious Wolfram, who has an evil plan to steal valuable hero tech. Together, Midoriya and his classmates must fight to save the island and its inhabitants.

My Hero Academia: Heroes Rising

Year Released: 2019

Taking place after season 4, Heroes Rising follows Class 1-A as they are sent to Nabu Island for a hero work experience. The island has been peaceful, and the students help with minor tasks. However, things take a dark turn when the villain Nine, who possesses multiple Quirks, attacks the island in search of a child with a Quirk he needs to cure himself. Class 1-A must band together to fight off Nine and his powerful crew, with Midoriya and Bakugo facing Nine in a dramatic and high-stakes battle for the island's survival.

My Hero Academia: World Heroes' Mission

Year Released: 2021

In World Heroes' Mission, an anti-Quirk cult called Humarise, led by Flect Turn, believes that Quirks will lead to humanity’s extinction. They release a deadly toxin that causes Quirk users to lose control of their powers. In response, Pro Heroes and U.A. students are deployed worldwide to stop Humarise's bombs before they detonate. Midoriya, Bakugo, and Todoroki are sent to the fictional country of Otheon, where Midoriya is framed for a mass murder. They must evade capture, defeat the cult, and stop the deadly plot to save the world.
