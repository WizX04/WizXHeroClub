Seasons 1-7

A Hero's Journey (Season 1)


I was born without a quirk in a world full of heroes, but my dream was always to become one like my idol, All Might. After a chance encounter with him, I found out that I could inherit his power, One For All. Enrolling in U.A. High School was a dream come true, and it opened my eyes to what it truly means to be a hero. I faced challenges I never thought I could handle, from the intense entrance exam to our first hero training exercises.


As I trained with my classmates, I learned that being a hero takes more than just power; it requires courage, determination, and heart. The bonds I formed with my friends and rivals were crucial, and every test pushed me closer to my goals. I realized that the path to becoming a hero would be filled with obstacles, but I was ready to face them head-on.


Come with me as I discover what it really takes to be a hero, from starting at U.A. High School to learning the hard way that power alone isn't enough. Get ready for an intense journey filled with new friends, challenges, and the realization that every step forward brings new tests.

Deku's Quirk (One For All)

Deku

Quirk: One For All

My name is Izuku Midoriya, but most people know me as Deku. I’m the ninth user of a quirk called One For All—a power that changed my life forever. It’s no ordinary quirk; One For All is unique because it can be passed down from one person to the next. With each new user, the quirk grows stronger, storing up more and more power. What’s incredible is that it doesn’t just give me immense strength, speed, agility, and endurance—it also lets me access the abilities of those who came before me.

Deku

Quirk: One For All

As I’ve trained and worked to master One For All, I’ve started to unlock the quirks of past users, like Blackwhip, which lets me control energy tendrils, and Float, which allows me to levitate. But there’s something even bigger about One For All—it was created to stop a terrifying quirk called All For One, which lets its user steal and control other quirks.

Deku

Quirk: One For All

I never thought I’d be someone who could inherit such a powerful ability, but thanks to my hero, All Might, I was given this incredible responsibility. Now, it's my turn to carry the torch and fight to protect the people who need it most.

Sports and Rivalries (Season 2)


The second season was incredible! We had the U.A. Sports Festival, where we showcased our abilities in front of the world. Competing against my classmates, especially Bakugo and Todoroki, drove me to my limits and made me realize how much I needed to grow. The thrill of the competition was exhilarating, and I was determined to prove myself as a hero worthy of One For All.


But the season took a dark turn when the League of Villains attacked during our U.A. School Trip. The realization that we were up against real threats made me understand the gravity of our training. I knew we had to protect each other and fight back, showing that we were not just students but true heroes in the making.


Gear up for the U.A. Sports Festival, where every hero student battles for a chance to shine! It’s not just about winning; it’s about growing, facing off against friends and rivals alike, and pushing beyond limits. Trust me, the competition is fierce—and the stakes get even higher when villains step back into the picture!

Deku's Rivals

Shoto Todoroki

Quirk: Half-Cold Half-Hot

My quirk, Half-Cold Half-Hot, allows me to generate ice from my right side and fire from my left. It gives me the ability to create massive ice walls or engulf enemies in flames, but I have to balance it carefully. Overusing one side too much can lead to freezing or overheating.


At first, I couldn't understand Deku. He pushed me to use my fire, the side I rejected for a long time because of my father. He sees strength in people where they don't see it themselves. I used to view him as just another obstacle, but now I realize he's someone I respect, and our rivalry drives me to become better. It's not about defeating him—it's about surpassing myself

Katsuki Bakugo

Quirk: Explosion

My sweat contains nitroglycerin, and I can ignite it to create explosions. The more I sweat, the bigger and more powerful my blasts become. It's great for combat and mobility, and with practice, I've learned how to control the size and force.


Deku... he's always been in my shadow, but then he started catching up, and that pisses me off! I used to think he was just some useless nerd, but now he's someone I can't afford to lose to. I still want to beat him, but not because I hate him—it's because I want to prove I'm the best. He pushes me to go further, and that's what rivals are for.

Tenya Iida

Quirk: Engine

My quirk, Engine, gives me engines in my legs that allow me to move at incredible speeds. With the right fuel, like orange juice, I can activate bursts of speed for both offense and rescue operations. It's all about precision and knowing when to accelerate.


Deku is someone I deeply respect, but our rivalry is not a typical one. It's born from admiration. I strive to be a great hero like my brother, and watching Deku's growth reminds me that I need to keep improving. He has a way of inspiring others with his determination and bravery, and while I may not have the same intensity in our rivalry as Bakugo, I see him as a motivator to better myself

The Rise of the Villains (Season 3)


In the third season, we headed to the U.A. Training Camp to sharpen our skills, but it wasn’t long before the League of Villains ambushed us. It was terrifying to confront such powerful enemies, but I knew we had to stand strong and protect one another. The experience taught me the importance of teamwork and resilience in the face of danger. It was a harsh reminder of the world we were stepping into.


Later, we participated in the Provisional Hero License Exam, a critical moment for my journey to becoming a true hero. Each challenge pushed me to my limits, and I felt the weight of my responsibilities. But everything changed when I confronted All For One, a villain with a dark past tied to All Might. I realized just how high the stakes were in this world of heroes and villains, and I knew I had to be ready for whatever lay ahead.


Things really heat up as our training gets interrupted by villains ready to strike when we least expect it! Follow me through an intense training camp, high-stakes battles, and the Provisional Hero License Exam, where I’ll face enemies like never before. The rise of villainy is real—and we must rise to meet it.

Rescue and Redemption (Season 4)


The fourth season was filled with intense emotions! Our mission to rescue Eri from the Shie Hassaikai, a yakuza organization led by Overhaul, became a defining moment for me. Eri’s quirk was being exploited, and I couldn’t stand by and watch. We faced overwhelming odds, but with the support of my classmates and pro heroes, I was determined to save her and prove that heroes can make a difference.


We also had a Cultural Festival at U.A., where we showcased our talents and grew closer as friends. It was a welcome break from the intense battles, allowing us to bond and enjoy our time as students. However, the battle against Overhaul was intense and eye-opening. It reminded me of the importance of teamwork, and the lengths we must go to protect those who can’t protect themselves.


Join me as we embark on a life-changing mission to rescue Eri from the clutches of a dangerous villain organization. The stakes have never been higher, and the emotional rollercoaster will leave you breathless. But it’s not all dark—watch us light up U.A. in the most memorable Cultural Festival yet!

Training and Transformation (Season 5)


The fifth season kicked off with the Joint Training Arc, where Class 1-A faced off against Class 1-B in a series of training battles. It was an amazing opportunity to push ourselves against our rivals and learn from each other. The friendly competition brought out the best in all of us, and I was excited to see how much we had all grown. Each battle tested our skills and strategies, helping us prepare for the challenges ahead.


But things quickly escalated when we became embroiled in the Paranormal Liberation War. The stakes were higher than ever as we faced off against the League of Villains and their new allies. I could feel the tension in the air, knowing that we were up against powerful enemies who would stop at nothing to achieve their goals. This season taught me the true meaning of courage and sacrifice, reminding me that as heroes, we must always stand together.


Look forward to epic showdowns as Class 1-A and Class 1-B go head-to-head in a series of battles that will test our limits and reveal just how much we’ve grown. But that’s just the beginning. As villains grow stronger, we find ourselves in the midst of a war that will change everything we know about being heroes.

War and Consequences (Season 6)


The sixth season dove straight into the heart of the Paranormal Liberation War. Watching my friends and fellow heroes fight against overwhelming odds was both inspiring and terrifying. Each battle was a test of our strength, willpower, and determination to protect our world. The gravity of the situation made me realize that the consequences of our actions would change everything, and I felt the weight of responsibility on my shoulders.


As the battles intensified, I faced my own challenges and learned more about my powers and the people around me. It was a harsh reminder of the realities we face as heroes. By the end of the season, everything changed for us in the hero world, and I knew we were entering a new era of challenges. But as long as I have my friends by my side, I’m determined to rise to the occasion and fight for what’s right.


Prepare for the biggest battle yet—the Paranormal Liberation War! Villains and heroes collide in a clash that will have devastating consequences. It’s a season of high-octane action, intense emotions, and the start of a new era in hero society. Every fight, every choice will shape the future we fight for.
